How it works

Search a game and we will show you what portion of players meet the system requirements for it.


Our application utilizes Steam Hardware & Software Survey data. We show you the global percentage of players who can run the game—a.k.a. whorunit™.



Global Playability Score


For games we calculate a global playability score based on the player percentage for satisfaction of the most important hardware components. Our score is on a scale of 0 to 100.
